Dan is a data scientist living in the Greater Boston area. He has worked for the past 3 years for Service Management Group (SMG). SMG is a consumer insights firm that works with large restaurant and retailers to measure customer experience.Recently, Dan has been a lead engineer and data scientist developing new solutions for automated text analysis used by SMG to understand customer survey responses. Dan has also been a principal engineer on SMG’s mobile development team and has worked closely with client teams to create custom insights for clients.

Prior to SMG, Dan worked for 10+ years at MIT Lincoln Laboratory in the Advanced Concepts and Technologies group. Dan wore many hats at MITLL leading teams taked with developing new solutions for the Department of Defense’s challenging problems. Dan’s focus was prototyping new systems (both software and hardware) and demonstrating their effectiveness in both simulation and field experiments. Dan’s contributions to these efforts were technical as well as in leadership.

Dan earned is BS degree from SUNY Fredonia State in Mathematics and has a MS and PhD from NC State. Dan’s dissertation at NC State focused on a nonlinear optimization algorithm called DIRECT.

Dan has developed technical skills in Python, many different machine learning algorithms, natural language processing applications as well as prediction, estimation and classification problems. Dan has also been a successful program manager, leader, mentor and has developed successful business strategies.

Outside of work, Dan enjoys running and spending time with his family in Dedham MA. Dan is originally from Grand Island, NY.
